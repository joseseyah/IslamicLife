About Us

Welcome to Islamic Life, a digital hub created with the aim of fostering a stronger sense of community and connection among Muslims in Manchester. We are passionate about providing a platform that brings together the diverse and vibrant Muslim community in this great city.

What We Offer

At Islamic Life, our mission is simple yet powerful: to empower Muslims in Manchester by providing a central space to discover and connect with their local Islamic community.

  • Mosque Locator: We understand the importance of finding a place to worship conveniently. Our platform offers an easy-to-use tool to locate the nearest mosques in Manchester. Whether you're a local resident or just visiting, we've got you covered.
  • Islamic Events: Stay informed about the latest Islamic events, lectures, seminars, and community gatherings happening in Manchester. Islamic Life is your go-to source for staying connected with the vibrant Islamic calendar in the city.
  • Halal Dining: Manchester boasts a diverse range of halal restaurants, and we're here to help you explore them all. Discover delicious options for halal dining, from local favorites to hidden gems.
  • Quranic Resources: For those seeking spiritual growth and understanding, we offer a complete copy of the Quran on our platform. You can access this holy text with ease, allowing you to engage with the sacred teachings of Islam.

We invite you to become a part of our growing community at Islamic Life. Together, we can build a stronger, more connected Muslim community in Manchester. Explore, connect, and engage with the resources we've provided, and let's work towards a more vibrant and cohesive Islamic presence in this wonderful city.

Thank you for choosing Islamic Life as your digital platform for all things Islamic in Manchester. We look forward to serving you and growing together as a community.

For any inquiries or suggestions, please don't hesitate to contact us. We're here to support and serve the Manchester Muslim community in the best possible way.
