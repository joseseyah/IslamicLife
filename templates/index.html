Why sign up to our community?

At the core of our mission is the commitment to provide a welcoming space for new Muslims and international students in Manchester. We recognize the challenges of adapting to a new culture, language, and faith, and our goal is to make this transition smoother by offering a platform where you can freely express your thoughts, share your experiences, and connect with like-minded individuals.

Learn More

Safe and supportive community

Joining our community means becoming part of a safe and supportive space where you can openly discuss your thoughts, questions, and experiences. Our members understand the complexities of embracing a new culture or faith, and together, we build a network of encouragement and understanding.

Share your journey

Provides a unique platform for new Muslims to share their personal stories. Whether you've recently embraced Islam or have been on this journey for a while, your experiences can inspire and resonate with others who may be facing similar challenges

Resource Hub

Access a wealth of resources tailored to the needs of new Muslims and international students. From guides on navigating the local community to tips for enhancing your spiritual journey
